Welcome to Thailand

'Siam' is the Southeast Asia country with a rich and diverse cultures, foods, and tourist attractions

Siam

Thailand is knowned as 'Siam', the country has it all from exotic cuisines, endless opportunity for adventure and the place is friendly people in the world.


CAPITAL CITY : Bangkok


CURRENCY : Thai Baht (See current exchange rates). $1 USD equals roughly 31 Thai Baht. You can often find cheap local meals for about 30-50 Baht ($1-2 USD), and an average beer at a bar can cost between 70-100 Baht ($2-3 USD).


LANGUAGE : Thai (In touristy areas, many locals speak English)


TRANSPORTATION : Getting around Thailand is super easy. Thailand has some wonderful public transport infrastructure in place. Main transport such as Skytrain (BTS), Underground train (MRT), public bus, Taxi, Uber, public motorcycle and Tuk-Tuk.
