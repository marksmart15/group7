The music festivals and parties in Colombia make it a paradise for enjoyment. You can celebrate the Barranquilla Carnival with its vibrant Cumbiambas and Papalleras, experience the Blancos y Negros Carnival in Pasto, or attend the Cali Festival where salsa dancing is a dream come true. Additionally, you can celebrate a Vallenato festival to immerse yourself in the historical sounds of the region or enjoy the melodious music played on the traditional Colombian instrument called the "tiple" by our talented rural farmers.
